AffAuto Video

AffAuto Video is an AI-powered desktop tool that helps affiliate creators create product videos, generate scripts, prepare captions, and upload videos to TikTok as drafts for further editing.

How it works

  1. The creator generates or selects a finished MP4 video.
  2. The creator connects their TikTok account using Login Kit.
  3. The app uploads the video to TikTok as a draft using the Content Posting API.
  4. The creator opens TikTok, reviews the draft, adds product links if needed, and publishes manually.
